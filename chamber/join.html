Membership Benefits

Non-Profit Membership

Our Nonprofit membership offers invaluable resources for nonprofit organizations, including directory inclusion, event access, and regular updates on city initiatives, all at no cost.

Bronze Membership

Stepping up to our Bronze Membership unlocks foundational benefits, such as online directory listings, exclusive networking mixers, and recognition in our quarterly magazine.

Silver Membership

For those seeking heightened visibility and engagement, our Silver Membership provides a significant boost, granting prime advertisement space on our website's homepage, priority sponsorship opportunities at major events, and amplified visibility through social media shoutouts.

Gold Membership

For businesses seeking premier exposure and extensive benefits, our Gold Membership is the pinnacle. Enjoy all the advantages of the Silver level, with added perks such as top-tier website advertisement placement, VIP access to exclusive events, speaking opportunities, and prominent features in our press releases and marketing materials.
